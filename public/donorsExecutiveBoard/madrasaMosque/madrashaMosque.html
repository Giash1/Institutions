Baitul Huda Jame Masjid (Madrasa Mosque)

Late Alhaj Abdul Motalib Saheb

Founder of Baitul Huda Jame Masjid

Former President of Poschimgaon Madrasha

Founder and Proprietor of Shohag Community Center, 91 New Eskaton Road, Dhaka-1000, Bangladesh

Philanthropy & Contributions

Alhaj Abdul Motalib Saheb was a highly respected, generous, and influential figure in society. He dedicated his life to social welfare, philanthropy, and community development. Among his notable contributions was the single-handed funding for the construction of Baitul Huda Jame Masjid (Madrasa Mosque), as well as the provision of a luxury flat (2002 sq. ft with parking) to support community initiatives.

His philanthropic efforts were recognized on significant occasions, including 10/03/2017 and 31/08/2022, which marked key milestones in his charitable projects.

Leadership & Service

Born on 1st January 1940, Alhaj Abdul Motalib Saheb played an active role not only in social work but also in political leadership. He served as the President of the Madrasha for a long period, during which he oversaw significant progress and development of the institution. His dedication to education and religious development left a lasting legacy in Dhaka and beyond.

Family & Legacy

He passed away on 1st February 2024, leaving behind a devoted family of two sons and two daughters.
