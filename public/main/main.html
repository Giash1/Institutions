  • Hifz Department
  • Hadith Department

Professional Staff

Our teachers are highly trained scholars who are dedicated to providing Islamic education at the highest level.

Deep Knowledge

We emphasize deep knowledge and understanding of Islamic subjects including Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, and more.

Dedicated Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to cover all essential areas of Islamic learning, helping students grow intellectually and spiritually.

Community Focus

Our institution plays an active role in the community by organizing events and services for all age groups.

Holistic Approach

We believe in a holistic approach to Islamic education that combines both religious and moral teachings.

Future Leaders

Our aim is to shape the future leaders of the Muslim community, well-versed in both religious knowledge and societal responsibilities.
