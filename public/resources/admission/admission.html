Admission to Poschimgaon Madrasha E Islamia Jamul Ulum

Welcome to Poschimgaon Madrasha E Islamia Jamul Ulum, where we provide a balanced education that integrates Islamic teachings with modern academic subjects. Our admission process is designed to welcome students who are eager to grow spiritually and intellectually.

Admission Timeline

Start Date: 1st of Ramadan

End Date: 15 days after Eid-ul-Fitr

Admission Fees and Costs

  • Admission Fee: 3500 Taka
  • Monthly Fee (All Departments): 1000 Taka
  • Boarding Cost: 2500 Taka (Discount available for orphan and poor students)

Why Choose Our Madrasha?

  • Comprehensive Islamic Education: Our curriculum includes Quranic studies, Hadith, Fiqh, and Arabic language courses to build a strong foundation in Islamic knowledge.
  • Modern Academic Subjects: In addition to Islamic studies, we offer courses in Mathematics, Science, English, and Computer Science to prepare students for the modern world.
  • Qualified Instructors: Our experienced and dedicated teachers ensure a high standard of education and personal guidance for students.
  • Conducive Learning Environment: We provide a safe, disciplined, and nurturing atmosphere where students can focus on their studies.
  • Scholarships and Financial Aid: We support students from diverse backgrounds by offering scholarships and financial assistance based on merit and need.

Admission Requirements

To enroll in our madrasha, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Submit a completed application form with all required documents.
  • Pass the entrance examination (for specific grade levels).
  • Attend an interview with the admission committee.

How to Apply

Interested students and parents can visit our madrasha for an application form or download it from our website. The admission office is available to guide you through the process and answer any queries.
