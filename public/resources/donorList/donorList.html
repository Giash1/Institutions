Become a Donor

We appreciate any support you can provide. Donors are crucial in sustaining our educational programs and improving our facilities. If you are living outside of Bangladesh, we encourage you to join our efforts.

Donor Criteria

As per the decision of the Poschim Gaon Madrasha Committee Board, individuals who wish to become donors must meet the following criteria:

  • Reside outside of Poschim Gaon, Cewdala, and Dokhinpara.
  • Commit to donating monthly or annually.
  • Support the mission and values of our Madrasha as a dedicated well-wisher."
