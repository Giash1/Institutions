Exam Terms and Norms

Terms of the Exams: Three terms

Norms of the Exams: Written and Oral exams

Exam Timing: Zilkod (زيلكاد), Safar (سفر), and Saban (سابان) (for all departments)

Marking System

  • 🎯 **Full Marks**: 100
  • 🏅 **Mumtaj (ممتاز)**: 80 and above
  • 🥇 **Jayeed Jiddan (جيد جداً)**: 65 and above
  • 🏆 **Jayeed (جيد)**: 50 and above
  • 🎖️ **Makbul (مقبول)**: 33 and above
